Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

You can view your detailed test results by navigating to your Profile page. There you'll find:

  • Overall performance statistics and trends
  • Subject-wise breakdown of your scores
  • Progress tracking over time
  • Comparison with other users
  • Detailed analytics for each test attempt

Your leaderboard ranking is calculated based on multiple factors:

  • Contest Rating: Your overall performance score across all tests
  • Improvement Rate: How much you've improved over time
  • Consistency: Regular participation and stable performance
  • Difficulty Level: Performance on harder questions carries more weight
  • Rankings are updated in real-time after each test completion

We offer a comprehensive range of preparation materials:

  • Mock Tests: Full-length placement exam simulations
  • Topic-wise Tests: Focused tests for specific subjects
  • Speed Tests: Quick assessments for time management
  • Practice Sessions: Unlimited practice with instant feedback
  • Previous Year Papers: Real placement test questions from top companies
  • Adaptive Tests: Questions that adjust to your skill level

Our platform provides comprehensive progress tracking tools:

  • Daily, weekly, and monthly study streaks
  • Subject-wise progress charts and analytics
  • Personal goal setting with deadline reminders
  • Achievement badges for reaching milestones
  • Study time tracking and recommendations
  • Performance comparison with your past attempts

  • Browser: Use the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
  • Contact: Reach out via WhatsApp or Email for immediate assistance
  • Our technical support team responds within 30 minutes during business hours

We value your input and have multiple channels for feedback:

  • Use the Feedback page to submit detailed suggestions
  • Rate individual tests and practice sessions
  • Join our community forums for discussions
  • Contact us directly via email with improvement ideas
  • Participate in our monthly user surveys
